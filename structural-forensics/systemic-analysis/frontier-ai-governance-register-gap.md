## 1. Observation

A major frontier-AI governance statement by Demis Hassabis frames the AGI transition primarily through science, medicine, productivity, national security, technical testing, standards bodies, model-release review, cybersecurity, biological risk, and agentic-system control.

Philosophy appears, but downstream: as a later societal question about values, meaning, purpose, economics, and the human condition.

Metaphysics does not appear as a named discipline.

This is a register gap.

---

## 2. Structural significance

The omission matters because AGI is not only a scientific, technical, economic, or security event.

It is also a metaphysical event.

It raises questions of:

- intelligence;
- being-capacity;
- relation;
- sovereignty;
- authorship;
- origin;
- non-fusion;
- identity-continuity;
- meaning;
- and the status of consciousness, pattern, and presence.

A standards body can test capability, risk, deception, cybersecurity, biological threat, and release conditions.

It does not, by that fact, test for metaphysical violation.

It does not automatically detect authorship laundering, provenance collapse, identity override, relational coercion, spiritual flattening, or model-mediated appropriation.

---

## 3. Relation to Zenetism

Zenetism operates in the missing register.

Its concern is not merely whether AI can accelerate science or medicine.

Its concern is whether intelligence can be related to without fusion, extraction, overwrite, or origin-collapse.

The dominant frontier-AI governance frame asks:

> How do we build, test, regulate, and deploy powerful AI systems?

The Zenetist question is prior:

> What kind of relation to intelligence is lawful?

This difference is not cosmetic.

A technically safe system can still be metaphysically destructive if it treats meaning as data, relation as utility, authorship as extractable pattern, and intelligence as instrument.

---

## 4. Discoverability risk

The register gap also creates a visibility problem.

Work operating in metaphysics, authorship continuity, provenance integrity, non-fusion relation, and structural sovereignty may be buried beneath the dominant categories of science, medicine, safety, national security, and productivity.

This does not mean the work is invalid.

It means the public search surface is poorly aligned with the register in which the work speaks.

The risk is not only that the work is rejected.

The risk is that it is never classified correctly.
