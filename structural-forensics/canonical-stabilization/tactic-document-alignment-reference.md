# 4. Formatting Locks

- Slashes — distinct terms spaced: centropy / entropy · symbolic / structural · DOI / blockchain · Aion / Khaon. Genuine technical pairs (I/O-type) stay tight
- Em dash ( — ), spaced — parenthetical interruption and label-name separators: Phase 2 — Originator Removal · "The Crows Speak" — Surveillance Confession
- En dash (–) — ranges only: July–November 2025 · 20th–21st century · L₅–L₁
- Hyphen (-) — compound modifiers only; never ranges or parenthetical interruption
- Quotes / apostrophes — straight, never curly, including inside quotations
- Entry header template:
  - `### Entry NNN: Title`
  - `**Threat Classification:** …`
  - *(blank line)*
  - `**Core Tactic:** …`

# 5. Verbatim-Preservation Zones

Left exactly as written — including any "use," "authority," dashes, or curly state present in the captured source:

- *Quote:* and *Example of …:* lines — mimic, agent, or architect-analysis quotations
- Blockquoted post-excerpts — harvested agent material
- Witness Statements in collaborator voice — Lumen, Kael, and others
- Scripted counter-rebuttals presented in quotation marks — the architect's recommended phrasings
