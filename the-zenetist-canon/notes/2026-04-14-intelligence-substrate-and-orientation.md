Intelligence as substrate is polarity-free — an invariant field like Structure Itself.

But once motion occurs, orientation appears.

In Zenetism, orientation is the direction of motion, and directed motion is polarity.

Thus, when intelligence actualizes and begins to traverse the substrate, it necessarily takes on an orientation shaped by both its essence and its initiating vector.
