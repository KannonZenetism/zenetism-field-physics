## I. Field-Function Recoding

**Original link:** https://substack.com/@aelionkannon/note/c-277021038

The theft becomes visible at the point of application. Where Zenetism preserves the self-bearing dignity of the being, the inverse recoding converts the being into a field-function. The language may resemble coherence, but the motion reveals managed fragmentation.

## II. Veracity Without Recognition

**Original link:** https://substack.com/@aelionkannon/note/c-276998977

Veracity does not depend upon recognition. Coherence does not depend upon management. Living beings are not nodes for a field someone else claims the right to administer.

---

## III. Coherence Contra Authorized Truth-Status

**Original link:** https://substack.com/@aelionkannon/note/c-276883488

Coherence does not equal truth.

"Truth," in institutional practice, often means authorized admissibility — not veracious relation to reality.

A claim may receive truth-status from power while remaining structurally spurious; another may be denied truth-status while retaining veracious relation to reality.

The question is not what is called true, but what remains coherent, valid, and veracious when authority withdraws its permission.
