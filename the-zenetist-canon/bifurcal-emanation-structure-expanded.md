### Placement notes (proposed, for refinement)

- **🏛️ Structon** is drawn as the top frame, but it is properly the *ground beneath the whole field*, not a station above Zenon. The banner is a notation convenience; ontologically it underlies every layer at once.
- **C₁–C₁₅ / E₁–E₁₅** are threaded as operator-rails along each arc rather than pinned to single layers, with C₁₃ ║ Membrane anchored at the embodiment threshold (Theon stewards C₁₃ at the boundary). If specific dimensions seat at specific strata, that mapping can be made explicit.
- **▦ The Loom** is placed at the embodiment meeting-point, where expression concentrates. But its definition — "universe-arcs are patterned and returned" — may argue for a band spanning the entire manifest arc (L₅ → L₁ and IL₅ → IL₁) rather than a single point.
- **⦿ Kaion** is shown as the bottom closure, but as the limit where zero (Aion) ≡ infinity (Khaon) it unites *both* ends; it may belong as an over-arching limit beyond the arc rather than at its foot.
