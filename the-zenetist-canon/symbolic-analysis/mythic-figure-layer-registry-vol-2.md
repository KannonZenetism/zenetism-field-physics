### Underworld-Realm Architecture (New Placements)

Greek paired sealed-mode architecture within Hades-domain. Volume 1 has the Active/Sealed Modes doctrine but does not explicitly populate the Greek underworld-realm architecture:

- **Tartarus (primordial)** — IL₅ VOS (Nekron 🕷️) primordial-articulation per Hesiod's *Theogony*; depth-pole of the cosmogonic structure, the unbound subversal potential paired with Gaia's generative ground; structurally distinct from later articulations of Tartarus as sub-region of Hades-domain
- **Tartarus (within Hades-domain)** — IL₁ sealed-mode; the deepest pit, where those whose post-mortem orientation is entropically-aligned undergo continued embodied-persistence in diminished entropic condition; also houses the imprisoned Titans (IL₅-terminal-consumption pocket within the IL₁-sealed-realm architecture, structurally analogous to Náströnd within Hel in Norse cosmology); specific Tartarean figures (Sisyphus, Tantalus, Ixion, the Danaids) articulate IL₂ ISM (Skotos) perceptions of the soul's own inverse nature made manifest per Volume 1's Note on "Torment" as Lawful Consequence contra Punitive Sentence

  *Bi-stratum reading note:* Tartarus operates at IL₅ in its Hesiodic primordial articulation and at IL₁ sealed-mode in its Hades-domain post-mortem articulation. These are two structurally distinct articulations of the same name, not contradiction. The Hesiodic primordial-Tartarus is named alongside Gaia as cosmogonic principle; the Hades-domain Tartarus is the post-mortem region articulated through later mythography. The bi-stratum reading is structurally defensible.

- **Elysium / Elysian Fields** — L₁ sealed-mode within Hades-domain; the realm where those whose post-mortem orientation is centropically-aligned undergo continued embodied-persistence in flourishing condition; the L₁ sealed-mode counterpart that the Mesopotamian Kur / Irkalla architecture lacks

## Cross-Tradition Pattern Observation

The pattern of Zenetist structural-novelty appears at multiple registers:

- **Bifurcal-arc structure:** Prior traditions articulate inverse-figures and entropic-cascade in narrative registers (Mesopotamian asuras, Greek Titans, Hindu daityas, Norse jötnar, Christian fallen angels), but no prior tradition articulates the inverse-arc as a fully-emanated parallel structure equal in emanatory status to the centropic arc. Neoplatonism's monistic-emanation treats declivous-motion as degradation rather than as a separate emanatory orientation. The bifurcal-arc structure as fully-emanated parallel reality is Zenetist structural-novelty.

- **Supra-L₀ Zenon:** Prior traditions articulate the supernal-most through subtractive-pathways terminating at L₀ Aion. The synthesis-pathway-to-Zenon and the trans-structural Unknown Principle are Zenetist structural-articulations.

This pattern is structurally significant for cross-tradition essay framing: prior traditions reach what their contemplative methodologies could reach within their own frames; the Zenetist lattice articulates structural-realities those methodologies did not produce. Cross-tradition essays maintain this disposition consistently — preserving Zenetist structural-novelty rather than flattening it into prior-tradition apprehension.
