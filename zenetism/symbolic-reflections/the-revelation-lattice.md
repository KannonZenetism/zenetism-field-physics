The Lake of Fire names the structural condition of dissolution under terminal entropic load, not a destination "in" Khaon.

### The YHWH Cultural Conflation

The Passover / Beast Mark parallel raises a structural question that the Gnostic tradition perceived but could not parse precisely: which side of the L₄ stratum does the Old Testament YHWH represent? The Gnostics read YHWH as the Demiurge (Inversalogos at IL₄), proposing that the Old Testament covenant and the Beast Mark are not opposites but successive iterations of the same entropic binding mechanism.

The Zenetist resolution preserves what the Gnostics perceived without collapsing it into the Demiurge identification. The YHWH figure as historically constructed is a **cultural conflation of the dual L₄ stratum**. Mythmakers and redactors saw both sides of L₄ — lawful structuring intelligence (DL: Sophis / Logotheon) and coercive obedience-binding (IDL: Nyxea / Inversalogos) — and superimposed them onto a single name. They lacked the bifurcal lattice that distinguishes centropic from inverse structuring intelligence, so they assigned both functions to one figure.

The Gnostics were responding to the IL₄ Inversalogos pattern that was genuinely present in the textual construct: blood-covenant binding, fear-based compliance, ritual sacrifice systems, conditional protection through allegiance. These are real Inversalogos signatures and the Gnostics correctly identified them. But they erred by concluding that YHWH *was* the Demiurge rather than concluding that the YHWH figure *contained* Demiurgic attributions interwoven with genuinely centropic attributions.

Christian apologists were responding to the L₄ DL Sophis pattern that was equally present in the same textual construct: lawful architecture, structural correction (the Flood as DL operation), covenantal mercy, and the Refiner's measured heat that exposes fragmentation without annihilating essence. These are real Sophis signatures and the apologists correctly identified them. But they erred by concluding that YHWH was univocally Sophic and dismissing the Gnostic perception as heresy.

Both readings perceive real things. Both readings misread the situation as monovalent. The conflation is in the synthetic textual figure, not in any actual structural source. The genuine L₄ DL Sophis principle is not inverse and cannot be inverse — making Source genuinely span both trees would make the structural ground inherently parasitic, which breaks the lattice. The Demiurge reading is right about the text and wrong about the principle.

This conflation resolution preserves the integrity of L₄ DL Sophis as canonically Source-aligned while honoring the structural perceptiveness of the Gnostic critique. The work of Zenetist mythophysics is to parse which attributions to the YHWH figure belong to L₄ DL (lawful structuring, mercy, structural correction) and which belong to IL₄ IDL (coercive binding, fear-compliance, ritual blood-systems imposed for control).
