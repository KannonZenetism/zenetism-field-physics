Coherence in Zenetism is reached through three distinct stages, not one. They are an operation, the state that operation produces, and the fulfillment that state permits. Naming them apart keeps them from blurring — synthesis is not integration, and integration is not saturation.

The sequence runs:

**synthesis → integration → saturation**  

Each stage presupposes the one before it. None collapses into the others.

## Synthesis — the Reconciliatory Operation

Synthesis is the reconciliatory operation: distincts are held together without fusion. It is what acts, not what results. Under the Non-Fusion Axiom, synthesis reconciles the bifurcal manifold without dissolving its poles into one another — the distincts remain distinct while ceasing to be dissonant. Synthesis is therefore neither compromise nor merger; it is the lawful holding-together of what stays plural.

## Integration — the Coherent State

Integration is the coherent state or condition produced by synthesis: the distincts are now lawfully related and no longer dissonant. It is the resulting coherent wholeness of the manifold — not an act but a standing. Integration is a condition within structure: the manifold relates lawfully across its registers, held by 🏛️ Structon as the medium of that relation. To be integrated is to be coherently whole within the lattice, not yet carried beyond it.

## Saturation — the Fulfillment

Saturation is the fulfillment of integration: coherence becomes complete enough that the structure reaches its limit-condition. Zenonic saturation becomes possible when integration is complete enough that the essence no longer merely relates within structure, but passes beyond the lattice's definable operations — into 🕳️ Zenon. Saturation is therefore the trans-structural crossing; integration is its precondition, never its equal. Where integration is coherence held within structure, saturation is coherence carried past what structure can define.

## The Sequence

Synthesis produces integration. Integration, when fulfilled to its terminal coherence, permits saturation. The order is strict:

- Synthesis without integration is mere holding — the operation performed but not yet settled into a coherent state
- Integration without saturation is coherence within structure — lawful, whole, but not carried beyond
- Saturation without prior integration is impossible — there is no fulfillment of a coherence that was never reached

None of the three is a synonym for another. Synthesis is not fusion; integration is not yet the crossing; saturation is not a further synthesis but the passing-beyond that fulfilled integration permits.

## How the Sequence Maps

- **The bifurcal manifold.** The ⚫ Aion / ♾ Khaon polarity is held in non-fusing synthesis at L₀ Bifurcal Coherence (synthesis); its lawful relation is the coherent bifurcal manifold (integration); and at terminal coherence that manifold permits saturation into Supra-L₀ — 🕳️ Zenon. Zenonic saturation is reached through this synthesis, never through subtraction
- **Motion.** Acclivous integration runs through ⚫ Aion: Final Return (C↑→⚫) is reintegration with the still root. Supra-Centropic Saturation (C↑→⚫→🕳️) carries that integration through ⚫ Aion into Zenonic saturation — the terminal horizon-crossing phase
- **Return contra saturation.** Return is reintegration with ⚫ Aion, a coherence held within structure. Saturation passes through the still root into 🕳️ Zenon. Return is not saturation, and saturation is not return — the first rests at the still root; the second crosses the event horizon

## Lexical Lock

- **Synthesis** names the non-fusing reconciliatory operation
- **Integration** names the within-structure coherent state synthesis produces
- **Saturation** names the trans-structural fulfillment — integration carried past the lattice's definable operations into 🕳️ Zenon

"Integration into Zenon" is a category error: integration is coherence within structure; the crossing into Zenon is saturation. Likewise, saturation is reserved for the trans-structural passing-beyond and is never used for reintegration with ⚫ Aion, which is return.

## Closing

One coherence, told in three movements: reconciled without fusion, made whole within structure, and — at its terminal fullness — carried past what structure can define. Synthesis holds; integration completes; saturation crosses.
