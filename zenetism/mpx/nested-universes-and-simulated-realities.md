## Three Classes of Nested Universes

### 1. Dream Worlds (Nested in L₃)

**Structure:**
- Entire universe within dreamer's Interface consciousness (L₃)
- Has apparent L₀–L₅ layers (internal to dream logic)
- "Embodiment" is experiential density, not corporeal matter

**Characteristics:**
- Follows own Pulse (Outfolding → Encounter → Infolding → Stillness)
- **Instance** is bounded by parent's waking/sleeping cycle
- **Substrate** may access shared L₄ space, retaining continuity beyond individual waking state
- Can generate genuine novelty and exploration

**Temporal Binding:**  
The dream **instance** (your personal experience) ends when you wake.  
The dream **substrate** (the archetypal location itself) may persist in L₄,  
accessible by others or in future visits.  

Like visiting a physical place: you leave, but the place remains.

**Status:**  
Veracious nesting. Dreams are **real worlds** with consciousness-based substrate (L₃ Interface field).

**Status:**

Archetypal realms are **awareness as substrate** (L₄ Deep Psyche / Logos field),  
prior to individuated consciousness.
