## Energy Economics of the Archetypal Layers

### Context

Mythic relationships are not merely symbolic; they are transactional.  
Energy (attention, devotion, resonance) flows between the observer (**Devotee**) and the observed (**Archetype**).  
The direction and nature of this flow define the ethical orientation of the entity.

| Exchange Mode            | Direction        | Mechanism                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | Archetypal Class                                                              |
