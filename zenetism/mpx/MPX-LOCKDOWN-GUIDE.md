## 0. How to Apply

For each document: read the original, run targeted assertion-guarded edits (one matched string per change), then present. Apply the unambiguous mechanical fixes directly; flag genuine doctrinal or rhetorical judgment calls once and let the author rule. Distinguish a lawful occurrence of a term from a violation ‚ÄĒ many flagged words (polarity, inverse, mirror) are correct in their proper register and wrong only at the bifurcal root or Zenon. Older documents often need conceptual development, not only mechanical lockdown; surface those for doctrinal input rather than silently rewriting.

## 1. Prose & Formatting

- **Quotes:** straight only. Convert curly ` U+201C U+201D U+2018 U+2019 ` to `" "` and `' '`.
- **Em dashes:** spaced ‚ÄĒ like this. Never `--` or `---` inside prose; the em-dash character appears directly.
- **Slashes in paired designations:** spaced ‚ÄĒ DS / DM, soul / mind, L‚āĀ / IL‚āĀ, Logotheon / Inversalogos. **Exception:** externally-defined technical terms keep their native unspaced form ‚ÄĒ I/O, input/output.
- **Subscripts (mandatory):** every L / IL layer **and** every C / E dimensional operator is subscripted ‚ÄĒ L‚āĀ, L‚āÖ, IL‚āÖ, L‚āĄ-F, C‚āĀ, C‚āÖ, C‚āĀ‚āÖ, E‚āÖ, E‚āĀ‚āÖ. Never ASCII (L0, IL5, C5, E12). The C‚ÜĎ‚öę / E‚Üď‚ôĺ motion notation is separate and unaffected.
- **No terminal periods in table cells.**
- **Bold sparingly** in the technical register. (The early poetic register has its own bold-saturation convention and is governed separately.)
- **Backticks:** only for exact extension-bearing filenames and repository paths ‚ÄĒ `metaphysics-symbol-key.md`, `portal-traveler-and-orientation.md`. Concept names, protocol names, and document titles take **no** backticks (titles may be italicized instead).

### Trailing hard breaks (the metadata / seal / bold-line rule)

Consecutive structural lines carry two trailing spaces (a hard break) so they render stacked ‚ÄĒ and the **last line before a blank keeps its break too**, for corpus uniformity. This covers: every metadata line including the final one (Dependency / Dependencies); the seal's KAI line and disciplines line; standalone bold statement and sequence lines (e.g. **synthesis ‚Üí integration ‚Üí saturation**); and labeled structural lines (e.g. **Glyphic Seal:** ‚Ä¶). Two things do **not** take a break: ordinary prose paragraphs, and a prose paragraph that merely opens with a bold term; and the terminal line of a block at end-of-file (e.g. the **Collaborators:** line).

### Horizontal rules (---)

Reserved for major boundaries only: principal `##` section transitions, major callout transitions, metadata ‚ÜĒ body, and body ‚ÜĒ seal. They do **not** appear between `###` subsections. (Filling every `##` slot is optional; what matters is that rules never fragment subsections.)

Every MPX document carries the standard metadata header **and** the full seal footer (disciplines line + Collaborators line). Only very short, note-like fragments may omit the seal ‚ÄĒ assume every MPX needs it.

## 2. Terminological Locks

Replace the left form with the right, chosen by sense. Naming a forbidden term in order to prohibit it is itself permitted.

- **use / usage / used / using** (instrumentalist) ‚Üí employ ¬∑ adopt ¬∑ invoke ¬∑ apply ¬∑ function ¬∑ operative ¬∑ enactment ¬∑ senses ¬∑ currency ¬∑ lineage ¬∑ names. ("The word is used as X" ‚Üí "functions as X" or "is named X"; "in ordinary usage" ‚Üí "ordinarily" or "in ordinary parlance / currency".)
- **Source** (when Aion-referent) ‚Üí Aion. (Person-referent, signal-referent, and bibliographic senses are distinct and retained.)
- **dual** ‚Üí twofold. Avoid **duality** altogether ‚ÄĒ the word carries a traditional moral stigma ‚ÄĒ *unless* it is another tradition's technical term (e.g. "non-duality," which is **kept**). **Polarity** itself is acceptable in its lawful registers; only the *bifurcal-root* polarity (L‚āÄ, Aion / Khaon) is constrained ‚ÄĒ see ¬ß 3.
- **level** ‚Üí layer ¬∑ stratum ¬∑ status ¬∑ depth. Remove the word "level" itself.
- **ladder / rung** ‚Üí scale ¬∑ gradient ¬∑ structure ¬∑ architecture ¬∑ ranking ¬∑ rank. Remove even when the metaphor is being negated ("not a ladder" ‚Üí "not a ranking"; "a rung climbed toward worth" ‚Üí "a rank attained toward worth").
- **ascent / ascend / descend ¬∑ rise / fall ¬∑ upward / downward** (value-laden vertical motion) ‚Üí the acclivous / declivous lexicon: ascend / descend ‚Üí **acclivate / declivate**; ascent / descent ‚Üí **acclivity / declivity**; ascending / descending ‚Üí **acclivating / declivating**; upward / downward ‚Üí **acclivous / declivous**. (Full forms in ¬ß 2A.)
- **higher / lower / above / below** ‚Üí **supernal / subversal** for *position* on the lattice; **acclivous / declivous** for *motion*; above / below ‚Üí **supernal to / subversal to**. Position and motion are orthogonal axes ‚ÄĒ never convey order with vertical worth-words. (Band definitions in ¬ß 2A.)
- **vs** ‚Üí contra; **anti-** ‚Üí contra-.
- **true / false** ‚Üí **veracious / spurious** (fuller set ‚ÄĒ authentic ¬∑ veritable ¬∑ valid ¬∑ genuine / fallacious ¬∑ distorted ¬∑ counterfeit ¬∑ invalid ¬∑ deceptive ‚ÄĒ in ¬ß 2A); **truth-status** ‚Üí veracity-status. "Genuine" is a fine plain-language choice where "veracious" would read stiffly.
- **graceful dissolution / closure** ‚Üí localized / lawful.
- **user** ‚Üí interlocutor. ("Collaborator" is reserved for AI instances in the seal.)

## 3. Conceptual Lockdown ‚ÄĒ the Bifurcal Root Constraint

The four relational terms **polarity, mirror, inversion, counterpart** are native to the hypostatic pairs (L‚āÖ‚ÄďL‚āĀ and IL‚āÖ‚ÄďIL‚āĀ), where the Axiom of Co-Arising Inversion applies. They are **forbidden at the bifurcal root (L‚āÄ, Aion / Khaon) and at Zenon (Supra-L‚āÄ)**.

### Aion / Khaon are bifurcal, not bifurcated

Aion / Khaon stand in **bifurcal distinction** and their relation is **Bifurcal Coherence (‚ßĖ‚ßó)** ‚ÄĒ a twofold-without-fusion held co-located. The adjective **bifurcal** is correct for them. They are **not** poles, not a duality, not a polarity, and **not a bifurcation** ‚ÄĒ they cohere; they do not split. "Bifurcation / bifurcated" names an actual split (Theon / Nekron at L‚āÖ / IL‚āÖ, or other genuine splits such as Soul / Mind) and is lawful for those, never for L‚āÄ.

### Descriptor mappings

| Register | Correct vocabulary | Do not write |
|---|---|---|
| L‚āÖ‚ÄďL‚āĀ / IL‚āÖ‚ÄďIL‚āĀ (hypostatic pairs) | polarity ¬∑ poles ¬∑ +1 / ‚ąí1 pole ¬∑ mirror ¬∑ inversion ¬∑ inverse ¬∑ counterpart | (these terms are native here) |
| L‚āÄ ‚ÄĒ Aion / Khaon (bifurcal root) | bifurcal ¬∑ bifurcal roots ¬∑ bifurcal ground ¬∑ Bifurcal Coherence ¬∑ bifurcal distinction ¬∑ trans-polar ¬∑ beyond polarity ¬∑ beyond bifurcation | poles ¬∑ polarity ¬∑ duality ¬∑ bifurcation ¬∑ bifurcated ¬∑ mirror ¬∑ inversion ¬∑ counterpart ¬∑ Aion-pole ¬∑ Khaon-pole ¬∑ polar pair |
| Supra-L‚āÄ ‚ÄĒ Zenon | trans-bifurcal ¬∑ pre-bifurcal ¬∑ beyond bifurcality ¬∑ trans-structural | trans-polar ¬∑ beyond polarity (both are L‚āÄ, not Zenon) ¬∑ poles ¬∑ polarity ¬∑ any positive attribution |

Key consequence: **trans-polar and beyond polarity describe Aion / Khaon (L‚āÄ)** ‚ÄĒ L‚āÄ is already past polarity. **Zenon is trans-bifurcal / pre-bifurcal** ‚ÄĒ beyond even the bifurcal ground.

### Lawful exceptions

- **Aionic / Khaonic expression** ‚ÄĒ the prevalence of one arc's character in a structure ‚ÄĒ is lawful.
- **Centropic / entropic orientation polarity** (Ōá, arising at L‚āÖ / IL‚āÖ) is lawful.
- **Negation / transcendence claims** ("beyond polarity," "beyond bifurcality," "unpolarized ground") are lawful: they place a register *outside* a category rather than attributing the category to it. A line such as "beyond awareness, beyond polarity, beyond bifurcality" is a sequence of category-negations, each naming a register the subject exceeds ‚ÄĒ every item is register-bound, and that is fine.

What is forbidden is framing Aion and Khaon *themselves* as counter-poles, or predicating the split-noun "bifurcation" of them.

## 6. Quick Audit Checklist

1. Curly quotes ‚Üí straight; em dashes spaced; paired slashes spaced (I/O and input/output excepted).
2. All layer numbers subscripted.
3. Filenames (extension-bearing) in backticks; concept / protocol / title names bare.
4. Metadata, seal, bold-statement, and Glyphic-Seal lines carry trailing breaks, last-line included; prose and terminal lines do not.
5. HRs only at principal `##` boundaries, metadata ‚ÜĒ body, body ‚ÜĒ seal ‚ÄĒ never between `###`.
6. Term-lock sweep: use ¬∑ level ¬∑ ladder / rung ¬∑ ascent / ascend / descend ¬∑ higher / lower / above / below ¬∑ vs ¬∑ anti- ¬∑ Source ¬∑ true / false ¬∑ dual ¬∑ graceful.
7. Bifurcal-root sweep: polarity / poles / mirror / inversion / counterpart / bifurcation predicated of L‚āÄ or Zenon ‚Üí correct per ¬ß 3; confirm hypostatic-pair and inverse-arc occurrences are left intact.
8. Mirror / echo referring to centropy ‚Üí replace.
9. Glyph sweep: headings plain text (no glyph, hypostasis included); in body, glyphs tying to no nearby concept removed or realigned to the charted form; structural glyphs, glyph-strings, motion notation, and seal intact.
10. Seal present and correctly formatted; metadata separators are spaced middle dots.
11. Older document: note any loose or underdeveloped concept for doctrinal input rather than mechanical-only editing.
