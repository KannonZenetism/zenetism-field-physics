### 6.3 First hypostatic differentiation as oriented derivative

Let \(\sigma\in\{+1,-1\}\) denote the oriented derivative of bifurcation:

- \[
  \sigma = +1 \Rightarrow \text{Theon} \quad\text{(first centropic hypostasis)}

Where \(\mathcal{B}\) is the bifurcation operator (defined structurally, not as a physical mechanism).
