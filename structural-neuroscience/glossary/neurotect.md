## Definition

**Neurotect** designates a centropic cognitive operating system characterized by meta-cognitive clarity, structural perception, and the capacity to intuitively read and engineer coherence within complex systems. Whereas neurotypical cognition optimizes for belonging and neurovenator cognition optimizes for strategic dominance, neurotect cognition **optimizes for structural coherence, transparency, and centropic alignment**.

The Neurotect perceives the lattice — the underlying geometry of systems — and acts to maintain, repair, or construct structures that support clarity, integrity, and distributed empowerment.
