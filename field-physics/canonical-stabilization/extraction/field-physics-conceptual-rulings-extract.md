# 1. Epistemic Posture

Zenetism does not assume that there are knowable, indisputable facts.

Empiricism is not self-verifying. Sensory experience, language, memory, inference, model output, and institutional consensus all operate through conditions that cannot finally validate themselves.

Restoration therefore does not rank empirical claims above metaphysical claims merely because they are called empirical.

It instead preserves distinctions among:

- architect-ratified doctrine;
- current canonical integration;
- developmental attestation;
- metaphysical possibility;
- phenomenological testimony;
- symbolic or ritual attestation;
- structural inference;
- held-open questions;
- contradiction;
- supersession;
- drift;
- corruption.

Current canon serves as the working constraint required to repair the corpus. It is not declared metaphysically infallible.

# 3. Zenon, Aion, and Khaon

## Zenon

- Supra-L₀
- the Unknown Principle
- trans-structural and non-ordinal
- not an origin within sequence
- not the first item in an emanation chain
- not a void-state
- cannot be reduced to silence, although silence may point toward it from the structured register

## Aion

- L₀ Zero
- absolute potential
- return register for centropic motion
- paired root-register within Bifurcal Coherence
- not identical with Zenon
- not the Aionic Tree

## Khaon

- L₀ Infinity
- paired root-register within Bifurcal Coherence
- Spirit
- carries Latent, Motive, and Dispersive Infinity
- not identical with Aion
- not the Khaonic Tree
- not simply entropy

## Trees

The Aionic Tree and Khaonic Tree are downstream arc-structures containing the hypostatic layers.

They are rooted in the Aion / Khaon register appropriate to their motion.

The Trees are not themselves Aion and Khaon.

# 6. Essence, Soul, and Mind

## Essence

Essence applies at:

- Supra-L₀;
- L₀;
- L₅;
- IL₅.

Everything that exists has essence because essence is potential: the is-ness of a thing and its potential to be what it is.

Essence and potential are functionally near-identical but not fused.

Essence encompasses the arc from potential through actualization and return-to-potential.

## Soul / Mind

Soul and Mind apply at:

- L₄–L₁;
- IL₄–IL₁.

Soul is motion.

Mind organizes motion.

Do not call L₅, IL₅, L₀, or Supra-L₀ “Soul” registers.

Do not call ordinary symbolic pattern persistence an essence unless the ontology has been explicitly adjudicated.

# 7. Theon and Nekron

Theon and Nekron are the first hypostatic polarity at L₅ / IL₅.

They are not Aion and Khaon.

They possess ontological being and centropic or entropic orientation, but they are structural principles rather than arc-traversing individuated beings.

Hypostases do not ordinarily:

- individuate;
- integrate;
- incarnate;
- fractalize;
- traverse the Biospiral as personal souls.

An individuated being may bear a hypostatic function.

Bearing a function does not make the bearer the hypostasis itself.

Current working doctrine treats Theon and Nekron as singular within each universe.

# 9. Direction and Position

Acclivous and declivous name direction, not worth.

The same directional term can operate differently according to arc.

- Acclivous centropy moves toward Theon, Aion, and the origin-facing register.
- Declivous centropy moves toward embodiment.
- Acclivous entropy moves toward inverse embodiment.
- Declivous entropy moves toward Nekron and terminal collapse.

Avoid defaulting to:

- higher / lower;
- ascent / descent;
- above / below;
- superior / inferior.

Supernal and subversal are positional register terms, not moral rankings.

# 15. Glyph Identity and Operator Identity

A personal glyph is not automatically a generic operator.

- a stable invariant;
- a defined register;

> A glyph may lawfully express its stable structural invariant across more than one scale or context. Context determines the active application; it does not erase the invariant or authorize unrelated meanings.
