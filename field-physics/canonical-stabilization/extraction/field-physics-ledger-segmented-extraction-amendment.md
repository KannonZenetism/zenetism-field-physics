# Amendment Purpose

The original handoff plan required the revised ledger, ten stabilization-package files, and one foundational chat document to enter a single extraction session.

Practical context limits make that architecture unworkable for a foundational document of approximately 350 pages.

This amendment replaces single-session extraction with a segmented, artifact-carried process.

---

# Revised Handoff Rule

The ten-file stabilization package remains the full verification and adjudication apparatus in the Field Physics canonical-stabilization folder.

It is not part of the default Liora extraction upload set.

Liora receives:

1. `field-physics-foundational-extraction-capsule.md`
2. one controlled source segment
3. the cumulative findings ledger
4. a compact prior-document deduplication index when reviewing Chat Document 2

Individual FP files or full registers enter a session only when requested for a specific comparison.

---

# Source Segmentation

Each foundational chat is divided at natural boundaries into segments targeting 15,000–20,000 words, with 25,000 words as the ordinary maximum.

Segment identifiers:

- `CD1-S01`, `CD1-S02`, ...
- `CD2-S01`, `CD2-S02`, ...

One fresh high-effort chat is opened for each segment.

---

# Continuity Mechanism

Continuity is carried by the cumulative findings ledger rather than conversational memory.

Finding IDs remain continuous across segment chats:

- `FIND-CD1-001` onward
- `FIND-CD2-001` onward

The ledger records:

- completed segments
- source ranges
- findings
- duplicate families
- continuing threads
- next finding number

---

# Extraction Burden

During segmented extraction, Liora is required to cite applicable:

- `MAP-FP##-###` rows where clear
- `OQ-...` open questions
- `CORR-###` candidates
- `CDP-###` dependencies

Detailed audit flags and full glyph, synonym, and numerical normalization may be added during Lumen's verification pass.

A finding is not invalid because exact current coverage could not be decided from the compact capsule.

Use:

> Current Coverage: Verification required

where necessary.

---

# Completion Sequence

1. Complete all CD1 segment extractions.
2. Consolidate and deduplicate `FIND-CD1` in a source-free consolidation session.
3. Create a compact CD1 deduplication index.
4. Complete all CD2 segment extractions with the deduplication index present.
5. Consolidate and deduplicate `FIND-CD2`.
6. Transfer both findings ledgers to Lumen for full-package verification.
7. Conduct targeted conceptual adjudication.
8. Draft approved restorations only after Aelion's rulings.
9. Perform post-restoration conceptual and adversarial audit.

---

# Governing Principle

The full package preserves nuance in the repository.

The compact capsule preserves only what extraction requires.

The cumulative findings ledger preserves continuity.

No single conversation is required to contain the entire audit architecture and the entire foundational record simultaneously.
