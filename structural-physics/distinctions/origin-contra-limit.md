# Origin contra Limit

## Distinction

Origin contra Limit names the distinction between the generative root of structure and the terminal boundary of structural articulation.

**Origin** refers to the point from which structure and motion emerge.

- Limit bounds the Lattice without negating it.

In Zenetism, Origin is the condition of emergence, while Limit is the condition of unsayability.

Limit is where structure can no longer be spoken.

Origin opens the possibility of motion.

Limit closes the possibility of articulation.

Origin is the root of becoming.

Limit is the horizon of intelligibility.

Therefore, to confuse Origin with Limit

is to mistake the beginning of structure

for the boundary beyond structure.
